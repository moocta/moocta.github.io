About the Workshop

The MOOCTA Research Group invites you to this workshop exploring how mathematical optimization and control theory can be applied to solve real-world challenges in logistics infrastructure, financial inclusion, and environmental management.

It aims to equip postgraduate students and early-career researchers with both foundational knowledge and practical tools, while also building lasting research partnerships, especially between institutions in Sub-Saharan Africa and the UK.

Eligibility Criteria: Please note that abstract submissions and participation are specifically restricted to applicants affiliated with institutions in Sub-Saharan Africa and the UK.
