Workshop Announcement: Mathematical Optimization and Control 2026

Posted: March 2025

The MOOCTA Research Group is proud to announce the upcoming workshop on Mathematical Optimization and Control for Industrial and Societal Challenges in Sub-Saharan Africa.

Date: 7 – 11 September 2026
Venue: Future Africa, Pretoria

The workshop will focus on applying mathematical optimization and control theory to real-world problems in logistics, finance, and environmental sustainability. It will serve as a platform for collaboration between researchers, industry experts, and students from Sub-Saharan Africa and the UK.

For more information, please visit the Workshop 2026 page.
