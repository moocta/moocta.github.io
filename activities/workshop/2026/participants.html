Speakers

The workshop will feature a Keynote Speaker from South Africa and Invited Researchers from Spain, the UK, China, and Poland. These experts bring extensive knowledge in heuristic optimization, stochastic analysis, and mathematical modeling for industrial and environmental processes.

Target Audience

We expect to host a diverse group of participants, including academic researchers, industry practitioners, and postgraduate students from South Africa, the UK, and other Sub-Saharan African countries.
