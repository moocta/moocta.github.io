Venue & Accommodation

Future Africa, Pretoria

Date: 7 – 11 September 2026

Address: Future Africa, University of Pretoria, South Africa

About the Venue

Future Africa is a collaborative platform at the University of Pretoria designed to address Africa’s pressing challenges through transdisciplinary research. The venue provides comprehensive conference facilities and on-site accommodation in a secure environment.

Location & Accessibility

Located in Pretoria, the venue is accessible from OR Tambo International Airport, facilitating travel for international and regional participants.

More Information

For venue-related information, please visit the Future Africa website. If you have any inquiries regarding the workshop or accommodation, please direct them to us via our Contact page.
